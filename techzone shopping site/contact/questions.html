Do unpacked products enjoy the right to warranty?

Yes. Unpacked products enjoy the right to warranty according to the warranty certificate issued by eMAG. The warranty does not cover the same period as the packaged brand new product.

Can unpacked products be returned?

Yes. Unpacked products are used with the same return conditions as new unpacked ones.

The product broke down. How do I proceed to have it fixed?

Check if the warranty certificate is valid. If you have received a Warranty Certificate from eMAG, you must fill in the Product Repair Form to be picked up by a courier and delivered to our service center.

Can I bring the product for repair in person?

Products that require repair will only be shipped through our couriers.

I am not satisfied with the product. How do I proceed to get it back?

Check that the product is eligible for return and then fill in the Product Return Form, but no later than 30 calendar days after receipt / receipt. The product must be returned in the same condition in which it was delivered.

I do not have a certificate for warranty in parcels. How can I receive it?

The warranty certificate is issued in electronic format and can be found in your account in the "Order History" section. For products with warranty certificates issued directly by the manufacturer, you will be able to find it in the box of the respective product upon delivery.

How soon will I receive my money from the returned product?

The amount will be refunded to you within a maximum of 14 days after completing the Product Return Form. The money will be transferred to the bank account you specified in the form. If you paid by card, the value will be returned to the card you paid with.

How to check if the product can be returned within 30 days?

The product can be returned if it is in the same condition in which it was delivered (no scratches, damage, incomplete set of accessories, damaged packaging / box, electric shocks) and is not part of the exceptions listed here.

How long do I have to pick up my shipment from the courier company's office?

For shipments delivered with the courier company Econt, you have 7 working days to pick up your shipment from their office. For shipments delivered by courier company Speedy, you have 7 calendar days to pick up your shipment from their office. For shipments delivered with the courier company Rapido, you have 14 calendar days to pick up your shipment from their office.

What are the benefits of ordering online?

Ordering online brings you a number of advantages: 1) It can be made at any time of the day and is processed automatically according to the order of its registration; 2) The reservation of the products is made automatically when sending the order, if the product has the status "Available"; 3) You do not wait in queues and you do not carry heavy bags and products to your home; 4) It's easy to track - by logging in to your account, you can check at any time what is the status of your order and where exactly it is located.
